Plot

Devil May Crie is a massive game that throws players into a world where archons rule over the land, and elemental visions are granted to worthy mortals. The game follows the dense main character called Traveler as they go on a quest to find their missing step-sibling and build their own harem with the strange Paimon. Armed with the power to tap into the elements without a vision, the Traveler breaks all the laws within Teyvat and performs near-impossible feats. To find their sibling, they must venture to all seven regions and learn the hidden truth surrounding the world.
